About dataset :

The dataset belongs to a retail company that collected some data from their customers,
whether or not they have purchased certain products. It consist of 4 columns Country 
(From customer belongs to), Age (Customer's Age), Salary (Salary of Customer), Purchased 
(whether they have purchased product from them).

About Tools :

1. NumPY : To work with arrays as input of dataset.
2. MatPlotlib : To plot the graph and charts. Here we will use Pyplot module, to plot the graph.
3. Pandas : for importing the datset, create the matrix of features and dependent variable vector.
4. Sklearn : To import SimpleImputer class from its impute module which would help us to handle the missing Data.
